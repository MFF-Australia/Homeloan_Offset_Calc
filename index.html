Description

Our mortgage calculator is designed to help you estimate your regular repayments based on your loan amount. You can adjust the repayment frequency to monthly, fortnightly, or weekly. The calculator provides estimates for both Principal and Interest repayments over your chosen loan term.

Please note the following assumptions used in our calculations:

  • Up-Front Fees: The calculator does not account for any potential up-front fees. Only ongoing fees are considered, excluding any initial or end-of-loan fees (such as discharge costs).
  • Interest Rate Stability: It is assumed that the interest rate remains constant throughout the loan term.
  • Interest Compounding: Interest is compounded at the same frequency as your selected repayment scheduleâ€”weekly, fortnightly, or monthly. In practice, the interest compounding frequency may differ from the repayment frequency.
  • Yearly Calculation Basis: For the purposes of calculation, a year is assumed to consist of 26 fortnights or 52 weeks, equating to 364 days instead of the actual 365 or 366 days.
  • Rounding: No rounding is performed during the calculation process. However, repayments are rounded to at least the nearest cent in actual practice.

These assumptions are designed to give you a clear estimate, but please consult with one of our advisors for a more detailed and personalized analysis.
